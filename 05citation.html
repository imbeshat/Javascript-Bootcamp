When somebody has said something and we ant to quote that person then citation is used.

The blockquote HTML tag indicates that the enclosed text is an extended quotation.
The q tag indicates that the enclosed text is a short inline quotation. Browser displays it by surrounding the text inside the quotation mark.
The cite tag is used to describe a reference to a cited creative work, and must include the title of that work.
The bdo tag is used to override the current direction of the text.

Examples:

They think it's all over
- by Kenneth Wolstenholme during TV commentary in the closing moments of the 1966 FIFA World Cup Final
